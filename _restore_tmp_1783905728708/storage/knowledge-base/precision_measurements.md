# 📐 Precision Measurements & Architectural Clearance Standards

This document establishes the official millimeter-level dimensional guidelines for Spacious Venture interior architectural layouts. Precise clearances are mandatory to prevent structural conflicts and ensure functional utility.

---

## 🍳 Modular Kitchen Dimensions

| Cabinet Element | Standard Dimension (mm) | Tolerance Range (mm) | Description / Placement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Counter Height** | 850 | 820 - 880 | From finished floor level (FFL) to top of countertop stone. |
| **Countertop Depth** | 600 | 600 - 620 | Standard depth. Must clear standard dishwasher and drawer runners. |
| **Backsplash (Dado)** | 600 | 450 - 650 | Vertical distance between countertop surface and bottom of wall cabinets. |
| **Wall Cabinets Height**| 600 | 600 - 750 | Height of wall-mounted cabinets. |
| **Wall Cabinets Depth** | 350 | 300 - 380 | Shallow depth to prevent users from bumping their heads while working. |
| **Chimney Height Gap** | 650 | 650 - 750 | Clear gap between cooktop grate and bottom of chimney filter. |
| **Top Loft Height** | 600 | 450 - 900 | Upper cabinet tier running from wall cabinets to ceiling slab. |
| **Top Loft Depth** | 600 | 560 - 600 | Deep storage tier aligning flush with base counter below. |

## 🛋️ Living Room TV Unit & Rafter Clearances

1. **TV Placement Height**:
   - The center of the television screen should be positioned at **1050 mm - 1100 mm** from finished floor level (FFL) to align with average seated eye-level.
2. **Floating Console Clearance**:
   - The floating TV console bottom must have a clear gap of **200 mm - 300 mm** from the floor for clean floor sweeping and Robot Vacuum access.
   - Console depth must be exactly **350 mm - 400 mm** to accommodate media boxes.
3. **Vertical Rafter (Fluted Slats) Sizing**:
   - Individual rafter width: **30 mm - 50 mm**.
   - Individual rafter thickness: **12 mm - 18 mm**.
   - Groove spacing (gap between slats): **15 mm - 25 mm**.
   - For flush concealed doors: The door panel leaf must have matching rafter spacing. A **3 mm** gap tolerance must be maintained on door edges so the seam is invisible.

---

## 🛏️ Bedroom Wardrobe Standards

1. **Wardrobe Depth**:
   - Standard swing door wardrobe depth: **600 mm**.
   - Sliding door wardrobe depth: **650 mm** (includes 50 mm track runner gap).
2. **Hanging Rail Clearances**:
   - Coat/Dress hanging clear height: **1500 mm - 1600 mm**.
   - Shirt/Suit hanging clear height: **1000 mm - 1100 mm**.
3. **Loft Separation**:
   - Wardrobe lofts start at **2100 mm** height (standard door lintel level) and extend to the ceiling slab.

---

## 📏 General Architectural Clearances

- **Primary Passage Walkway**: Minimum **900 mm - 1200 mm** clear width.
- **Secondary Passage Walkway**: Minimum **750 mm** clear width (between bed edge and wall).
- **Door Swing Clearance**: Ensure door leaf swing path is completely clear of wardrobe handles, counters, and sanitary fixtures.
