# 🙏 Pooja Mandir (Temple) Design Standards

This document establishes the traditional Vastu-compliant layout and material specifications for Pooja Mandir units in Spacious Venture contemporary residences.

---

## 🧭 Vastu Alignment & Positioning

- **Directional Orientation**: Vastu dictates the Pooja Mandir must be placed in the **North-East (NE)** corner of the residence.
- **Worship Direction**: The deity images or idols must face West or South so that the worshipper faces East or North during prayer.
- **Cabinet Heights**: The pedestal or platform holding the idols must be raised at least **300 mm - 450 mm** from finished floor level, ensuring deity feet align with chest height when kneeling or sitting.

---

## 🎨 Materials & Detailing

1. **CNC Teak Wood Lattice (Jali)**:
   - High-precision CNC routing on teak wood panels to create traditional geometric or floral lattice designs.
   - Used for side screens or folding shutters to allow light and ventilation.
2. **Backlit Stone Panels**:
   - Translucent white onyx or quartz backdrops backlit with warm white LEDs.
   - Laser-etched patterns (e.g. Sanskrit mantras, lotus petals).
3. **Storage Cabinets**:
   - Bottom drawers finished in dark teak laminate for keeping brass lamps, incense, and prayer mats.
   - Groove handleless profiles to maintain neat fronts.
