# ðŸ“– Comprehensive Interior Design Lingo & Material Dictionary

Vocabulary reference for Spacious Venture's design models.

### ðŸªµ Cabinetry & Wood Work Lingo

1. **Fluted Paneling (Rafters)**: Repeated vertical wood slats or half-round dowel moldings creating ribbed, linear texture. Used for feature walls, TV unit backdrops, or wrapping door panels.
2. **Concealed Flush Door (Hidden Door)**: Door leaf on concealed 3D hinges, sitting flush with wall panel. Covered in vertical fluted rafters, door joints disappear making door completely invisible.
3. **Groove Profile (Gola Profile / Finger Pull)**: Continuous metal profile (J-type or C-type) integrated into cabinet carcass. Enables handleless door operation via recessed channel.
4. **Tandem Box Drawers**: High-end double-walled metal drawer systems with soft-close runners for heavy kitchen utensils.
5. **Dado (Backsplash)**: Vertical wall space between kitchen countertop and upper wall cabinets, usually tiled or clad in marble.

### ðŸª¨ Stone & Surface Materials

1. **Book-Matched Statutario Marble**: High-end white Italian marble with bold, dramatic grey veins. "Book-matched" = adjacent slabs polished on opposite sides so veining patterns mirror like an open book, creating symmetrical design.
2. **Waterfall Edge Countertop**: Stone slab extends vertically down side of cabinetry/island to floor, creating continuous stone wrap.
3. **Quartz Surface**: Engineered stone (93% quartz crystals + resin). High stain resistance, uniform pattern. Alternative to marble.

### ðŸ’¡ Lighting & Ambience Lingo

1. **Cove Lighting**: Indirect lighting in ledges, recesses, or high valances. Light source hidden, reflects off ceiling/wall for soft diffused glow.
2. **Backlit Panel**: Strip LEDs behind decorative panel (marble or wood) facing wall, creating glowing halo effect around panel edges.
3. **Task Spotlights**: Directed downlights (usually 3000K warm white) over working areas like kitchen countertops or sink basins.
4. **Profile LED Strips**: Slim aluminum profiles housing LED strips with diffuser covers, routed flush into cabinet bottoms or wardrobe shelves.
