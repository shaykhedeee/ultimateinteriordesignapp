### 5. Elevation drawing maturity
Add:

- sheet index page

### 6. BOM → BOQ → cutlist continuity
Add:
- exact material yield assumptions
- hardware rule linkage
- edge band detail
- production presets
- estimate-line reconciliation
- cutlist export

## Critical rule

All of the above must still derive from the same reviewed geometry and scene version.
That is the main strategic advantage of StudioOS and should never be broken.
