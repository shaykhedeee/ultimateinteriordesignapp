Primary User Flows product journeys

Lead to render

User starts in CRMLeadDashboard, creates a project, briefs in ClientBriefStudio, receives floor plans, enhances them in FloorPlanAnalyzerScreen, builds layout in InteractiveCADScreen, selects materials in MaterialCatalogScreen, renders in Render3DStudio, and exports drawings/quote.

CAD to production

User draws walls/openings/modules in CAD, assigns finishes, generates drawings/elevation/RCP in DrawingsElevationsStudio, optimizes sheets in CutlistNestingScreen, spec ceilings in CeilingStudio, builds TV unit spec in TvUnitGenerator, and tracks jobs/timeline.

AI-assisted design

User opens CommandCenterScreen, selects workspace mode, uses AURA chat, photo edit, quick generate, and specialist tools. AI detects objects, assigns products, creates moodboards, and prepares render-ready states.

Command Center Workspace Modes role switching

Designer Mode

Optimized for interior designers. Focus on CAD, floor plan, drawings, renders, cutlist, ceiling, TV unit, and smart project pipeline. Emphasizes planning, drawing, and execution.

Designerexecution heavy

Brand Mode

Optimized for furniture and material brands. Focus on product config, materials, renders comparison, laminate swaps, photo edits, and invoice/BOM workflows.

Brandproduct + SKU heavy

Real Estate Mode

Optimized for real estate developers and brokers. Focus on quick generate, photo edit, renders, walkthroughs, timelines, and marketing-ready visual outputs.
