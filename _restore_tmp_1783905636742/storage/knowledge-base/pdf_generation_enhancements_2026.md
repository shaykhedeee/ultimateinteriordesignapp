# Professional 2D Millwork Elevation PDF Drafting Guidelines (2026 Update)

This document outlines the enhanced PDF blueprint rendering standards implemented in ULTIDA StudioOS.

---

## 1. 📏 Dimensioning & Witness Lines
* **Continuous Witness Lines**: Extension/witness lines are drawn continuously from the cabinet boundary/plinth corners directly to the dimension line offsets to ensure that text labels never float in empty space.
* **Double-Tiered Bottom Dimensions**:
  * **Tier 1 (Cabinet Runs)**: Displays individual cabinet module widths in a continuous chain.
  * **Tier 2 (Overall Run)**: Displays the overall wall carcass length directly below the individual chains.
* **Oblique Slash Ticks**: AutoCAD-compatible 45-degree angle tick slashes are rendered at the intersection of all dimension lines and witness lines.

---

## 🚪 2. Shutter swings & specialty compartments
* **Center-Split Door Swings**: V-swing dashed indicators for double shutters wider than 500mm split exactly at the center of the module.
* **Diamond Loft Swings**: Overhead loft cabinets display diamond-shaped dashed swing markers instead of classic V-swings.
* **Specialty Compartments**:
  * Clothes hanging slots render double-line clothes hanger rods.
  * Vanity/dresser areas render mirror frames with diagonal reflection lines (`//`).

---

## 🗄️ 3. Shelves & Handles
* **18mm Double Lines**: Internal shelves inside open cabinet modules are drawn as double lines representing the 18mm board thickness.
* **Solid Pull Handles**:
  * Drawers display thick, solid-filled horizontal rectangles.
  * Shutters display thick, solid-filled vertical rectangles.
