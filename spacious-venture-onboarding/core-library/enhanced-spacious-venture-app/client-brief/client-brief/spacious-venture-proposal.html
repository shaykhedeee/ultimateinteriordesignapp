03 — Walkthrough (Part I)

How It Works — Client Intake

A guided digital intake process to capture requirements during your first client meeting.

1

Open the Platform & Start Client Intake

Launch the application on your laptop or tablet. Input the client details and select their layout configuration (1BHK, 2BHK, 3BHK, 4BHK, or custom duplex/villa).

2

Configure Spatial & Lifestyle Parameters

Configure ceiling heights, family habits (kids/pets), cooking styles (heavy Indian / continental), gas configurations (LPG vs piped gas), and pooja orientation preferences.

3

Select Aesthetic Direction & Spaces

Select from 4 curated styles: Modern Luxury, Indian Contemporary, Scandinavian Minimal, or Bohemian Chic. Toggle which spaces are in-scope (Living, Modular Kitchen, Wardrobes, Pooja, etc.).

4

Upload Floor Plan & Reference Images

Drag-and-drop the builder's floor plan (PNG/JPG blueprint). Write specific requirements ("need breakfast counter in kitchen", "high-suction chimney required").
