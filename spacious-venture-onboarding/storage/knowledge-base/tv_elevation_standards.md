# TV Feature Wall & Dining Crockery Design Standards

This document outlines the standard specifications for Living Room TV Elevation Units and Dining Area Crockery units in Spacious Venture premium residential projects. All visualizers must conform to these layout details to guarantee consistency and high accuracy.

---

## 📺 TV Feature Wall Layout

1. **Backdrop Panel Finish**:
   - **Backlit Marble**: A large, centered book-matched Statutario white marble slab. It features integrated warm golden LED strip lights behind its edges, casting a soft glow.
   - **Wood Veneer**: Rich warm walnut wood veneer paneling with vertical grain.
2. **Concealed Flush Doors**:
   - Rafters (vertical wood slats/flutes) are used to cover the entire wall.
   - If a doorway or passage is situated on the same wall, the vertical wood rafters wrap seamlessly around the door frame and door leaf.
   - This creates a **seamless flush door** that blends completely into the rafter paneling and becomes invisible when closed.
3. **Fluted Paneling Limit**:
   - The fluted rafters should not run infinitely. They should terminate cleanly at the frame of the first door, transitioning into the backlit marble backpanel.
4. **TV Console Unit**:
   - A floating, low-slung TV console sits beneath the wall-mounted television screen.
   - Features rounded/curved edges and is finished in a light oak wood laminate matching the flutes.
5. **L-Shaped Sofa Proportions**:
   - Place a modern sectional sofa in a neutral beige fabric against the sofa wall.
   - Ensure the sofa ends exactly where the wall terminates, respecting room widths and leaving circulation walkways clear.

## 🍽️ Dining Crockery Niche Layout

1. **Upper Display Section**:
   - Suspended glass wall cabinets featuring dark smoked glass door panels with black aluminum frames.
   - Built-in warm LED spotlights inside the cabinets to highlight fine dinnerware.
2. **Center Niche**:
   - An open marble counter space (usually Statutario or quartz) for placing items or acting as a serving counter.
3. **Bottom Drawers**:
   - Configured with flat, handleless charcoal grey lower drawers.
   - Clean minimalist groove lines separate the drawer tiers, maintaining a sleek handle-free appearance.
