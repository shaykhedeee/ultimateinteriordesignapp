# 🛏️ Bedroom Wardrobe Design Standards

This document establishes the standard layout and material specifications for wardrobe cabinetry in Spacious Venture premium residential bedrooms.

---

## 📐 Layout Types & Clearances

1. **Swing Door Wardrobes**:
   - Best suited for large bedrooms with ample swing space.
   - Clear gap required in front of the wardrobe doors: **750 mm** (for a standard 450 mm door leaf swing).
2. **Sliding Door Wardrobes**:
   - Ideal for narrow passages or space-constrained master bedrooms.
   - Requires a double-track runner system. Total depth is **650 mm** (50 mm for the track, 600 mm carcass).
3. **Walk-in Closet (L-Shaped/U-Shaped)**:
   - Walkways between wardrobes must be a minimum of **900 mm** wide.

---

## 🎨 Materials & Finishes

1. **Smoked Glass Wardrobe Doors**:
   - Bronze or dark grey tinted tempered glass panels.
   - Slim black aluminum frames.
   - Internal vertical profile LED strips running on drawer shelves to light up clothing.
2. **Matte Acrylic / Laminate**:
   - Soft, anti-fingerprint surfaces in solid neutral colors.
3. **Fluted Wood Handles**:
   - Integrated wood handles running vertically from floor to loft level, creating a unified door design.
