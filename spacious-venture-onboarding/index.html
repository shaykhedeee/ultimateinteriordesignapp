Indian Ergonomic Sizing Standards for Media Walls

▪
Center Line Height: 1050mm to 1150mm from finished floor. Places screens center at direct comfortable eye level for sitting sofas.

Passageway Clearance: Minimum 900mm in front of consoles for active traffic flow.

Indian Ergonomic Sizing Standards for Modular Kitchens

▪
Countertop Height: 820mm - 850mm. Specifically lower than Western sizes (900mm) to minimize arm stress for standard Indian heights.
▪
Work Triangle: Sink (Water), Stove (Fire) and Fridge (Storage) distance should sum between 4.0m and 7.9m for smooth culinary traffic.
▪
Loft Clearances: Backsplash gap of 600mm enables high blender appliances without wall cabinet collisions.

Indian Ergonomic Sizing Standards for Wardrobes

▪
Swing Door Depth: 600mm. Accommodates standard shoulder hanger rods without shutter friction.
▪
Sliding Door Depth: 650mm. Requires an extra 50mm clearance to fit heavy overlapping door tracks.
▪
Indian Garments: Vertical rod clearances of 1100mm for Kurtas/Shirts; and 1600mm for floor-sweeping sarees or coats.
