2. Technical Product & Design Knowledge

To close premium home interior deals, you must speak with technical authority. Study these technical parameters and terminology to guide client material decisions.

Material Science: Gurjan Plywood vs. Particle Board

Material Type Spacious Venture Spec Why It Matters / What to tell the client
Plywood Core IS 710 BWP (Boiling Water Proof) Marine Plywood with Gurjan Core in wet zones. IS 303 BWR in wardrobes and dry zones. Aggregators use particle board. Particle board swells like bread in Bangalore's humidity—it crumbles within 2 years. Gurjan BWP plywood withstands direct water and lasts a lifetime.
Laminate Finish 1.0 mm thick high-gloss acrylic or premium matte PVC laminates. We do not use cheap 0.8 mm laminates that bubble or warp. Our 1.0 mm thickness ensures a flat reflection and high scratch resistance.
Modular Hardware Soft-close hydraulic hinges and tandem drawers from Hettich, Hafele, Ebco. We do not use local unbranded hinges that rust or sag. Every hinge is tested for 100,000 open-close cycles.

Finishing Quality: German CNC vs. Carpenter Hand-Cut

✗ Carpenter Hand-Cut
Hand saws leave micro-waves and uneven edges on plywood. Manual glue application peels away in 1-2 years due to moisture. Joint lines are thick and gather dust.
✓ German CNC Factory-Cut
CNC machines cut boards perfectly straight to 0.1 mm precision. Edge-banders melt PVC tape onto the board at 200°C under pressure, sealing it from water.
📐 Kitchen Work Triangle
The distance between the Sink (wash), Hob (cook), and Fridge (store) should be between 4 and 9 feet to minimize walking fatigue.
🧭 Vastu Shastra Basics
Kitchen: SE (Fire corner). Pooja: NE (Water corner). Master Bed: SW (Earth corner). Main entrance: East or North.

3. The Spacious Venture 8-Step Interior Process

Explain this structured timeline to your clients to set professional boundaries and show direct factory control over delivery.

1

1. Discovery Intake & Budget Analysis

BDA initiates contact, maps out BHK size, family profile (children, pets), Vastu requirements, and requests the floor plan on WhatsApp.

2

2. AI Concept Preview & Parallel Quote (24 Hours)

Design team inputs the floor plan into Spacious Venture Studio OS, generating custom 3D AI concept directions and an itemized module-by-module cost estimation.

3

3. Factory Walk-in & Material Inspection

Client visits our Sulikunte Road plant. BDA presents the materials checklist (IS 710 marine ply, Gurjan core, soft-close hardware) and demonstrates factory machinery.

4

4. 10% Booking & Laser Measurements

Client pays a 10% booking advance. Site engineers visit the property with digital laser meters to map column protrusions and pipeline configurations.

5

5. 2D/3D Design Freeze

Designer refines the 3D scene and exports 2D elevations showing every drawer depth and loft division. Client signs off on layout, materials, and colors.

6

6. 40% Production Payment & CNC Execution

Client pays 40%. Technical drawings are converted into CSV cutlists. Raw sheets are cut on German CNC panel-saws. (The **45-day clock begins here**).

7

7. Delivery & On-Site Assembly

Modular units are flat-packed and delivered via Spacious Venture trucks. Installation is quick, dust-free, and noiseless since all units are pre-drilled at the factory.

8

8. Handover, 10-Year Warranty & Care Activation

Site supervisor reviews a 50-point checklist. Keys are handed over, and the 10-year workmanship warranty and 2-year free maintenance care are activated.

6. Closing the Deal: From WhatsApp to Booking

Home interior contracts are high-value transactions. You cannot close this via phone calls alone. You must guide the client through the structured Spacious Venture conversion funnel:

1

Stage 1: The Initial Call

Goal: Establish micro-agreement and get them to WhatsApp their builder floor plan blueprint.

2

Stage 2: The WhatsApp AI Preview

Goal: Send their custom 3D AI concept renders and transparent initial estimate to build excitement within 24 hours.

3

Stage 3: The Factory Booking Close

Goal: Invite them to visit the Sulikunte Road plant to inspect materials, watch German CNC machinery cuts, and secure the booking advance.

How to Invite a Client to the Factory (Script)

BDA: "Hey [Client Name], I hope you saw the initial 3D AI preview and quote I sent you on WhatsApp. Most design firms ask you to sit in a retail showroom with artificial lighting and glossy catalogs. We do things differently. Since our factory is right on Sulikunte Road, Sarjapur, we want to invite you to do a Material Walkthrough. You can inspect the raw Gurjan plywood core, touch the soft-close drawer runners, and see the German CNC machines that will cut your actual units. It takes just 30 minutes. Are you free this Saturday morning at 11:00 AM, or would Sunday afternoon work better?"
🔒 The Factory Floor Close Technique
When the client is at the factory, show them a raw, unlaminated sheet of IS 710 BWP plywood. Have them touch the core. Explain: "Aggregators hide their core materials inside laminated panels. We show you our Gurjan plywood before we glue the laminates. That is why we offer a 10-year workmanship warranty and 2 years of free service care." Then, present the booking form: "To freeze your modular pricing and secure your 45-day completion slot, we need a 10% design booking advance. If you decide to cancel before the site measurements, this booking is 100% refundable."
